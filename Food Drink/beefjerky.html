Beef Jerky

Ingame description:
"Package of Beef Jerky. Tasty and pretty much last forever."

Beef jerky is a common food item in The Long Dark. Because of the amount of preservatives in it, it can never cause food poisoning, and decays extremely slowly.
Jerky can be found throughout every world, in containers or backpacks and inside buildings.
Stats:
Calories: 350kcal
Hydration: -10%
Weight: 0.10kg
